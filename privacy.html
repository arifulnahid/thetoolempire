The short version: All tools on The Tool Empire run entirely inside your browser. Your input data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers — because we don't have any servers processing your data.

01 Overview

The Tool Empire ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website located at thetoolempire.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

02 Data We Collect

We collect minimal, anonymous information to understand how our website is used and to improve the experience:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on page, referring URL — collected anonymously through Google Analytics.
  • Device data: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution — used only in aggregate, never linked to individuals.
  • Contact form data: If you submit our contact form, we receive your email address and message. We use this only to respond to your inquiry.

We do not collect names, addresses, payment information, or any personally identifying information beyond what you voluntarily provide in our contact form.

03 What We Don't Collect

Because all tools run client-side in your browser, we never see, store, or transmit:

  • Text you enter into any tool (word counter, JSON formatter, etc.)
  • Files you open or process using our tools
  • Colors, gradients, or design work created in our tools
  • Any calculations or results generated by our tools

This is by design. Your work stays on your device.

04 Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data such as page views, session duration, and general geographic region.

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish users. The data collected is aggregated and does not identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

05 Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Advertising revenue allows us to keep all tools free for everyone.

06 Cookies

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Help us understand visitor behavior in aggregate.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): Used to serve relevant advertisements.
  • Functional cookies: Used to remember preferences like dark/light mode if added in future.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some portions of our website may not function properly.

07 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We have no control over the content, privacy policies, or practices of those websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of every website you visit.

08 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

09 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Via our contact form
  • Email: privacy@thetoolempire.com
